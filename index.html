How it works

MCA database check Looks up Ministry of Corporate Affairs to confirm the company is legally registered in India.
02
GST validation Verifies the GST number format and cross-checks with the official GST portal.
03
Domain age & existence Checks whether the company website exists and how old the domain is — a key fraud signal.
04
Red flag keyword scan Scans the company name and description for fee-related words commonly used in job scams.
05
ML scoring model A Random Forest model combines all signals into a trust score and returns a final verdict.
